# Privacy Policy (GuideMe Travel)

**Last updated:** June 2026

GuideMe Travel ("GuideMe", "we", "our") provides AI-powered offline travel guides. This policy describes how we handle personal data under GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and India DPDP Act principles.

## Data we collect

| Data | Purpose | Legal basis |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Firebase Auth UID | Account and trip ownership | Contract |
| Trip origin/destination | Itinerary generation | Contract |
| Location (foreground + background during active trip) | Geofenced audio guides | Consent |
| Device storage | Offline maps and audio packs | Contract |
| Crash/analytics (optional) | App stability | Legitimate interest |

We do **not** sell personal data.

## Background location

GuideMe requests background location **only during an active trip** to play audio guides when you approach each attraction. Location is not tracked when no trip is active.

## Data retention

- Trip metadata: until you delete the trip or account
- Offline packs: deleted when you complete a trip or tap "Delete offline data"
- Server-side guide cache: anonymized POI content, shared across users

## Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting support. EU/UK users may lodge complaints with their supervisory authority.

## Third parties

- Google Firebase (auth, database, functions, storage)
- Google Maps Platform (places, routing)
- Google Cloud AI (Gemini, TTS, Translation)
- Wikipedia (CC-licensed factual content)
